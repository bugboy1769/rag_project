Eddard Stark is the head of House Stark and Lord of Winterfell. He governs the North.
Catelyn Stark is the wife of Eddard Stark. She advises him on political matters.
Robb Stark is the eldest son of Eddard and Catelyn. He is the heir to Winterfell.
Sansa Stark is the eldest daughter. She is betrothed to Joffrey Baratheon.
Arya Stark is the younger daughter. She owns a sword named Needle.
Bran Stark is a son of Eddard. He likes to climb walls.
Jon Snow is the bastard son of Eddard Stark. He feels isolated from the family.
Theon Greyjoy is the ward of Eddard Stark. He is essentially a hostage but treated well.

Robert Baratheon is the King of the Seven Kingdoms. He rules from King's Landing.
He is married to Cersei Lannister, the Queen.
Joffrey Baratheon is the son of Robert and Cersei. He is cruel and sadistic.
Jaime Lannister is the twin brother of Cersei. He is a member of the Kingsguard.
Tyrion Lannister is the younger brother of Cersei and Jaime. He is a dwarf known for his wit.
Tywin Lannister is the father of Cersei, Jaime, and Tyrion. He is the head of House Lannister.

Daenerys Targaryen lies in exile in Essos. She is the last known Targaryen heir.
Viserys Targaryen is the brother of Daenerys. He is abusive and wants the throne.
Khal Drogo is a powerful warlord of the Dothraki. He marries Daenerys.
Jorah Mormont is an exiled knight. He serves as an advisor to Daenerys.

Jon Snow joins the Night's Watch. The Night's Watch guards the Wall.
Samwell Tarly joins the Night's Watch. He becomes friends with Jon Snow.
Jeor Mormont is the Lord Commander of the Night's Watch. He mentors Jon Snow.
Tyrion Lannister visits the Wall to see the Night's Watch. He respects Jon Snow.

House Stark is an ally of House Baratheon.
House Lannister is a rival of House Stark.
The Night's Watch is neutral in political affairs.
